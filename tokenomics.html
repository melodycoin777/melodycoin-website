Token Distribution

MelodyCoin's tokenomics have been carefully designed to ensure fairness, sustainability, and growth. Below is a breakdown of how the token supply is allocated:

Category Percentage Description
Founder Allocation 5% Reserved for the project founder.
Band Donation Fund 30% Funds for donating instruments to schools.
Community Rewards 20% Rewards for token holders.
Developer Fund 15% To pay for ongoing development and innovation.
Liquidity Pool 15% Stabilizes the token for trading.
Marketing & Growth 10% Promotions, partnerships, and outreach.
Reserve Fund 5% Emergency fund for unforeseen expenses.
